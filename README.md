## About Me Hello! I'm an electronics enthusiast with a deep passion for understanding the world through the lens of 0s and 1s. My journey in programming began in 2017 with Arduino and C++, and since then, I've experimented with a variety of projects involving sensors, communication protocols (UART, SPI, I2C), and both digital and analog ICs. Over the years, I've explored IoT (Internet of Things) and "Talking Things" projects, combining hardware and software to create smart, interactive systems. I strongly believe that from the moment we're born, we start gathering data, and this data shapes who we are and informs the decisions we make. Driven by this belief, I have ventured into data science—what I consider the art of understanding and leveraging data to gain insights and solve problems. I am always open to collaborations in the fields of electronics, programming, IoT, and data science. Let's build something amazing together!
